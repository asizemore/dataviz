The above shows a type of multilayer network called a temporal network that represents a system that evolves over time. The temporal network can also be represented as a supra-adjacency matrix that records the presence of edges between nodes.

  • Hover nodes to see their location and connections in the supra-adjacency matrix.

  • Choose to color nodes by layer (time) or by node identity.

  • Drag the sliders to adjust the viewing angle.
